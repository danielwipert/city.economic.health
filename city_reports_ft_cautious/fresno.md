Fresno's economic indicators present a mixed picture, with some areas showing relative strength while others lag behind national trends. The unemployment rate stands at 7.9%, placing it at the lower end of the percentile rankings, which may reflect challenges in the local job market. The days on market metric stands at 59 days, which is relatively high, possibly indicating a less competitive market for homebuyers. Overall, the data suggests that while Fresno's economy exhibits some areas of growth, particularly in construction, it faces ongoing challenges in employment and labor force engagement.
