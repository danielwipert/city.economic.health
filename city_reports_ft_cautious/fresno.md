Fresno's economic indicators present a mixed picture, with some areas showing relative strength while others lag behind national trends. This growth in earnings contrasts with the relatively low weekly hours worked, averaging 33.9 hours, which ranks in the 24th percentile.

Despite this, the number of days properties remain on the market is 69 days, which is in the 42nd percentile, indicating a balanced market where neither buyers nor sellers have a significant advantage. The cost of living index is 5.07, ranking in the 40th percentile, suggesting moderate living costs relative to other U.S. cities.

Overall, these data points suggest a regional economy with areas of strength and areas needing improvement, without clear indications of broader economic trends or future directions.
