The Minneapolis-St. Paul-Bloomington metropolitan area ranks 16th among major U.S. metros, with a weighted percentile score of 38.9, corresponding to a grade of C+. These data points collectively suggest a metropolitan area with a stable but not particularly dynamic economic environment.
