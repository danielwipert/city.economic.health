# Hartford-West Hartford-East Hartford

**Grade: A+ (Excellent) | 68.8th percentile | April 2026**

---

Hartford earns an A+ grade, driven by its remarkably tight labor market with an unemployment rate of 2.70% and a labor force growing at a rate of 2.77% year-over-year. This robust labor market, combined with nonfarm employment growth of 1.88% year-over-year, makes it an attractive location for businesses looking to tap into a skilled and growing workforce.

The city's labor market strength is offset by stagnant wage growth, with hourly earnings increasing by only 0.13% year-over-year, a bottom-tier performance. This raises concerns about the sustainability of the labor market's strength and the potential for workforce attraction and retention challenges. The housing market is also slowing, with days on market increasing by 30.0% year-over-year to an average of 39 days, and a decline in building permits of 12.70% year-over-year, indicating a potential housing supply squeeze.

Hartford's strong job growth and low unemployment rate make it an excellent location for talent acquisition and expansion. However, stagnant wage growth and a potential housing supply squeeze pose significant risks to its long-term economic viability and attractiveness to businesses and workers alike, with the housing market showing signs of slowing and potential affordability issues.
