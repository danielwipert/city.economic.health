# Virginia Beach-Chesapeake-Norfolk

**Grade: B- (Below Average) | 46.0th percentile | June 2026**

The Virginia Beach-Chesapeake-Norfolk metro area has an overall grade of B- with a composite score ranking it at the 46.0th percentile out of 50 US metros.
