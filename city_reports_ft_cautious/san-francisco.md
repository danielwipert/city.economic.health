Labour force participation is at 64.45%, ranking in the 52nd percentile, suggesting a moderate level of engagement in the workforce. Housing and construction metrics show varying trends. However, the housing price index has declined by 0.06% over the same period, placing it in the 18th percentile, which could indicate a cooling in the housing market. The price per square foot has decreased by 3.63%, ranking in the 10th percentile, further supporting this trend. The average days on market for homes is 30 days, in the 96th percentile, which suggests a balanced market where properties are selling at a steady pace.
