The economic data for the Riverside-San Bernardino-Ontario metropolitan area, centered around the primary city of Riverside, presents a mixed picture. Overall, the weighted percentile score of 31.4 and the grade of C+ suggest that Riverside's economic performance is below average relative to other metropolitan areas, with notable strengths in earnings growth offset by challenges in employment and housing market dynamics.
