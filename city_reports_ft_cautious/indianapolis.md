The economic data for the Indianapolis-Carmel-Greenwood metropolitan area suggests a mixed but generally positive performance across several key indicators. Weekly hours worked have decreased to 33.4 hours, ranking in the 12th percentile, which could indicate shifts in employment patterns or workweek structures.

Overall, the data suggests that Indianapolis-Carmel-Greenwood is performing above average relative to other metropolitan areas, with strengths in employment, earnings, and housing prices, though some indicators point to potential moderations in certain sectors.
