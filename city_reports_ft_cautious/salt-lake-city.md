# Salt Lake City-Murray

**Grade: A+ (Excellent) | 73.7th percentile | April 2026**

---

Salt Lake City earns an A+ grade, driven by its exceptional economic character, with a low unemployment rate of 3.40% and a high labor force participation rate of 72.77%, indicating a deep and engaged workforce. The city's nonfarm employment growth is a remarkable 1.83% year-over-year, signaling rapid job creation and an economically expanding market.

The city's office and professional worker share ranks in the 98th percentile, indicating a deep pool of specialized talent. However, the housing market is highly competitive, with days on market at 45 days, and no year-over-year change, posing a challenge for relocating workers.

Salt Lake City offers businesses a thriving labor market with access to a deep and skilled workforce, making it an ideal location for knowledge-economy businesses. However, the city's highly competitive housing market may pose a risk for talent attraction and retention, as relocating workers may face significant challenges in securing housing quickly and affordably.
