**Grade: C+ (Poor) | 43.7th percentile | April 2026**

This suggests that San Antonio-New Braunfels is not well-suited for businesses requiring a deep knowledge-economy talent pool, such as tech or finance companies. The San Antonio-New Braunfels metro area offers businesses a unique combination of affordability and labor force growth, making it an attractive location for companies seeking to balance labor costs and talent attraction. However, the single biggest risk or constraint is the low labor demand and declining housing supply, which may limit the city's ability to accommodate rapid business expansion and attract high-skilled workers.
