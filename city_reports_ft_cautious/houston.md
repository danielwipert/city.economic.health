The unemployment rate of 4.30% is below average, ranking in the 30th percentile, which means the labor market has some slack but is not overly loose. However, this also indicates a less competitive housing market.

**Office Economy**
With an office/professional worker share composite score ranking in the 80th percentile, Houston-Pasadena-The Woodlands has a deep talent pool suited for tech, finance, consulting, and HQ decisions. This makes the city an attractive location for businesses in these sectors but less ideal for those in industrial or logistics-dominant economies.

The Houston-Pasadena-The Woodlands metro offers businesses a unique combination of affordable living, a deep professional talent pool, and genuine labor demand expansion, making it an attractive location for certain types of businesses.
