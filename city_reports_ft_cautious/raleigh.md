# Raleigh-Cary

**Grade: A+ (Excellent) | 76.5th percentile | April 2026**

---

Raleigh's A+ overall grade is driven by its exceptionally tight labor market, with a 3.00% unemployment rate and a 67.67% labor force participation rate, making it a highly competitive hiring environment. This environment poses significant challenges for businesses in attracting and retaining top talent.

Raleigh's job growth is strong, with nonfarm employment increasing by 1.25% year-over-year, indicating a healthy and expanding economy. However, the city's housing market is slowing, with days on market increasing by 4.5% year-over-year, which could stabilize the market given the strong employment growth and aggressive housing supply expansion, with building permits rising by 38.82% year-over-year.

Raleigh offers a highly skilled and engaged workforce, ranking in the 96th percentile for professional workforce concentration. However, the intense competition for labor may drive up wages and recruitment costs, posing a primary risk for businesses. Overall, Raleigh is an attractive location for knowledge-economy businesses and HQ locations, but companies must be prepared to navigate a highly competitive labor market.
