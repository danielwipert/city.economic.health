# Philadelphia — Economic Overview (Cautious FT Style)

## Polished, cautious FT-style column

The labour force participation rate of 63.96% is in the 42nd percentile, which may reflect a moderate level of engagement in the workforce.

The average days on market for homes is 53 days, which is in the 54th percentile, suggesting a balanced market.
