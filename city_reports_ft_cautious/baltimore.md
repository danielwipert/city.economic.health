The Baltimore-Columbia-Towson metropolitan area ranks 22nd in a recent economic assessment, with a weighted percentile score of 49.4, placing it slightly below average. Overall, the data indicates a mixed economic picture for Baltimore, with some sectors showing signs of strength while others exhibit cautionary trends.
