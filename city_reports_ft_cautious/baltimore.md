The number of days properties remain on the market has stabilized at 45 days, a figure that is above average, suggesting a balanced but not overly hot market.

Overall, the data suggests that Baltimore's economy is performing modestly, with some strengths in housing appreciation and labor market stability, but also areas of slower growth in earnings and construction activity.
