The economic data for the Boston-Cambridge-Newton metropolitan area reveals a mixed picture, with some indicators performing better than others. The unemployment rate stands at 3.9%, placing it in the 54th percentile nationally, suggesting a relatively stable labor market. The pace of sales, as measured by days on market, is 47 days, which is relatively quick and sits in the 70th percentile, suggesting a balanced market where properties are selling at a steady rate.
