St. Louis, ranked 23rd among metropolitan areas, exhibits a mixed economic profile as of the latest data. The median days on market for homes is 49, in the 62nd percentile, suggesting a balanced market where properties are neither selling extremely quickly nor languishing. The price-to-square-foot ratio, up 2.89% year-over-year, is also in the 86th percentile, indicating steady growth in property values. Overall, the weighted percentile score of 56.9 and a grade of "B" suggest an average economic performance, with strengths in housing and cost of living offset by moderate labor market indicators and a decline in new construction permits.
