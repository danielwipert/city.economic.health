# Louisville-Jefferson County

**Grade: C+ (Poor) | 39.7th percentile | April 2026**

This decline in labor force supply signals a structural headwind for hiring, making it more challenging for businesses to find and recruit new employees.

This suggests a slower and more accessible market for workers relocating to the city, giving them more time to find and purchase a home.

**Office Economy**
The professional talent pool in Louisville-Jefferson County is relatively shallow, with a percentile rank of 15th.
