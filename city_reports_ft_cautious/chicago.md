The labor demand composite score of 4.22 is near the median, reflecting this mixed picture.

The implication for businesses is a need to balance wage growth with productivity and pricing strategies to maintain profitability.

**Office Economy**

This city is less suited for businesses requiring a deep pool of tech, finance, consulting, or HQ talent, but may be more appropriate for industries with different skill requirements.

The Chicago-Naperville-Elgin metro area offers a business a location with moderate labor demand and wage growth, but significant challenges in labor force growth and housing supply.
