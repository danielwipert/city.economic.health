The Dallas-Fort Worth-Arlington metropolitan area ranks fourth in its economic performance, with a weighted percentile score of 51.2, placing it in the average category. Overall, the data indicates that the Dallas-Fort Worth-Arlington metro area maintains a stable economic position with strong labor market participation, though some sectors, particularly housing, show signs of moderation.
