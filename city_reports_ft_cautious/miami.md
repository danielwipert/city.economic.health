# Miami-Fort Lauderdale-West Palm Beach

**Grade: B- (Below Average) | 49.5th percentile | April 2026**

The Miami-Fort Lauderdale-West Palm Beach metro area has an overall grade of B- with a composite score ranking at the 49.5th percentile out of 50 US metros. This rate indicates that the labor market has some slack, making it slightly easier for businesses to hire compared to tighter markets. This increase, coupled with the absolute level, indicates a slower market, which can be more accessible for workers relocating to the city, as they have more time to find suitable housing.

**Office Economy**
Miami-Fort Lauderdale-West Palm Beach has an office economy percentile rank of 68th, indicating a deep professional talent pool. This makes the city well-suited for businesses in tech, finance, consulting, and HQ decisions that require specialized knowledge-economy workers.
